Consulting

We believe traditional AWS consulting is obsolete. Instead of charging hourly to build the same thing over and over again from scratch, our goal is to capture the solutions to all common infrastructure problems as code, in the form of Infrastructure Packages.

We offer consulting for the setup & customization work it takes to get you up and running with existing Infrastructure Packages, as well as to develop new Infrastructure Packages. Let us know what kind of infrastructure you're trying to build, and if we don't have an Infrastructure Package for it already, we'd be happy to build it—and, of course, document it, test it, and support it.
